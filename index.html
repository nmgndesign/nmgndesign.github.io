ABOUTDESIGN

One of my main focuses when engaged in a design project is creating a design which addresses the process of “solving a problem.” Design problems can be solved by changing either the external design or the technical and structural aspect. Furthermore, I believe that designing in accordance with given circumstances (i.e. corporate identity, design strategy) is also a part of the problem-solving process. In other words, it is crucial to establish a clear understanding of the problem before creating a design accordingly. In my case, I have always focused on planning from the internal aspect of a product based on my problem-solving process for my works to exterior of products.

This process includes an interest in ergonomic design and sustainability through an understanding of the design manufacturing process and the physical feasibility of the material. All of these interactions are complex and sophisticated to complete a product. I attempted to include in this portfolio the design process from inside to outside.

Ultimately I try to show and demonstrate how design can be used as a powerful tool to redefine the world around us and creates a more enjoyable life for the future of humankind.
